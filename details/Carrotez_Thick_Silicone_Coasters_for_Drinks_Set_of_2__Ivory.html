Carrotez Thick Silicone Coasters

Keep your surfaces clean and your drinks safe with these durable, stylish coasters. Designed for everyday use in the kitchen, on desks, or at the coffee table, they offer reliable protection for all your mugs, glasses, and tumblers.

With an extra thick, 0.3 in (0.8 cm) design, these coasters provide a strong barrier against scratches, heat, and spills. Their non-porous construction makes them easy to clean and maintain, ensuring long-lasting hygiene and performance.

Each coaster features a lip to trap condensation, preventing water stains and sticky residue buildup. Whether you're enjoying a hot coffee or a chilled glass of water, they are the perfect accessory to keep your space looking fresh and polished.

These BPA-free coasters are safe for use in the dishwasher, microwave, and boiling water, making them ideal for both home and office environments. Made in Korea, they are crafted to the highest standards for quality and durability.
